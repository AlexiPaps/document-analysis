Non-Disclosure Agreement

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("NDA") is entered into on September 30, 2025, between Disclosing Party ("Discloser") and Receiving Party ("Recipient").

1. Purpose
The Discloser intends to share confidential information with the Recipient for the purpose of evaluating a potential business partnership.

2. Confidential Information
Confidential information includes all proprietary data, business plans, and intellectual property disclosed by the Discloser.

3. Obligations
The Recipient agrees to protect the confidentiality of the information and not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent.

4. Duration
This NDA remains in effect for three (3) years from the date of signing.

5. Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York.
