Legal Services Agreement

This Legal Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on September 30, 2025, by and between Client ("Client") and Legal Firm ("Firm").

1. Scope of Services
The Firm agrees to provide legal services to the Client, including contract review, legal consultation, and representation in negotiations. The services shall be performed with due diligence and in accordance with applicable laws.

2. Payment Terms
The Client shall pay the Firm a retainer fee of $5,000 upon signing this Agreement. Additional fees for services rendered shall be billed hourly at a rate of $300 per hour, payable within 30 days of invoice receipt.

3. Confidentiality
The Firm shall maintain the confidentiality of all information provided by the Client, except as required by law or with the Client’s consent.

4. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days’ written notice. Upon termination, the Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the date of termination.

5. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California.
